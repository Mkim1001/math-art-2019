math*art 2019

about this course

This is a class about math and art. It's my hope that some knowledge of math can expand your artistic practice and that some knowledge of art can expand your sense of what math is.

On a very practical level, this course aims to give you familiarity with the tools that will make it easier to make technologically oriented art. This class aims to get you comfortable with thinking mathematically, and will introduce you to mathematical ways of thinking that will make it a lot easier for you to make digital art of all kinds. You'll learn to code in javascript or python and more importantly will learn to think programmatically. You'll learn the math behind pixels and sound and electronics. You'll learn to think algorithmically and understand how to successfully hack together whatever it is you want to do using readily available open source tools.

We'll also look at ways that math is actually a lot like art, art that is inspired by math, and make a whole lot of trippy visualizations. The goal, as always, is to have fun and learn a lot along the way.

Grading

Most of your grade for this class will be based on the daily assignments. If you work reasonably hard and come to class you should be able to finish those assignments in class.
